Junior Golf Training Programs

The Michael Karanga Golf Foundation is committed to propagating golf among young, upcoming players—both male and female—across Kenya. Our training programs reach over 1,000 students and are led by dedicated coaches, players, and staff.

  • MKGF August Holiday Training Program (3 weeks: 4th–22nd August)
  • Damacrest Schools Training Program
  • MKGF Kiambu County Interschool Training
