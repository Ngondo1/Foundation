Consultation & Professional Services

The Michael Karanga Golf Foundation offers a range of professional consultation services to support individuals, organizations, and events. Our expertise includes:

  • Digital Marketing: Social media management, online campaigns, and brand growth strategies.
  • Graphics Design: Creative design for posters, banners, event materials, and digital content.
  • Event Planning & Administration: End-to-end planning, logistics, and management for golf tournaments, corporate events, and community programs.
  • Golf Coaching & Clinics: Professional coaching for individuals, schools, and organizations.
  • Mentorship & Sponsorship: Guidance and support for elite golfers, including those from underprivileged and disabled backgrounds.
