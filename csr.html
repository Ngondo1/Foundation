Empowering Communities Through Golf

The Michael Karanga Golf Foundation is dedicated to making a positive impact through CSR initiatives and mentorship programs, fostering growth and opportunity for young golfers.

CSR & Mentorship Initiatives

The Michael Karanga Golf Foundation is passionate about giving back to the community and nurturing the next generation of golfers. Our CSR and mentorship initiatives include:

  • Inspirational Talks: Michael Karanga gives motivational talks to students in schools and during long holiday training sessions, inspiring them to pursue excellence both in golf and in life.
  • Free Golf Clinics: The foundation organizes free golf clinics, providing access to golf lessons and free golf balls for young aspiring players.
  • Sponsorship for Elite Golfers: We sponsor talented golfers, especially those from disabled backgrounds, giving them opportunities to compete and excel.

Motivational Talks
