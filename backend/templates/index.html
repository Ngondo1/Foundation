Our Mission

To inspire, educate, and empower youth through golf, fostering discipline, leadership, and excellence both on and off the course.

Our Immediate Initiatives

Twende Machakos na Karanga

Special outreach and golf event in Machakos to inspire and support local youth.

Twende Mombasa na Karanga

Bringing golf mentorship and equipment to the coast region’s aspiring young golfers.

Twende Nyahururu na Karanga

Community golf day and talent search in Nyahururu, open to all youth.

Junior Golf Clinics

Weekly training sessions for aspiring young golfers, led by professional coaches and mentors.

Scholarships

Financial support for talented youth to access top-tier golf academies and tournaments.

Community Outreach

Golf awareness campaigns and free introductory lessons in underserved communities.

Mentorship

Pairing young golfers with experienced professionals for guidance and career development.
