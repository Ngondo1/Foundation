Our History & Achievements

The Michael Karanga Golf Foundation was born from Michael Karanga’s inspiring journey—rising from humble beginnings at Ndumberi Golf Club to the pinnacle of amateur golf in Kenya. In 2018, Michael made history as one of the few amateurs to win a professional Safari Tour event at Limuru, igniting a passion to empower youth through golf.

Over the years, Michael has dominated Kenya’s amateur golf scene, clinching the Kenya Amateur Match Play Championship in both 2023 and 2025, and earning coveted slots at the Magical Kenya Open. His 2023 season was record-breaking: 13 wins out of 18 KAGC tournaments, including the Kenya Amateur Stroke Play Championship and the historic 88th Ghana Open in West Africa. These victories crowned him Kenya’s Golfer of the Year 2023 with an unprecedented 1,213 points—a testament to his consistency and excellence.

Michael’s achievements extend beyond individual events. He has set multiple course records and led Team Kenya to retain the Africa Region IV championship in 2023. His world amateur ranking soared into the Top 100, reflecting his growing global stature. The momentum continued in 2024 and 2025, with new titles like the Karen Challenge and Kiambu Open, and a successful defense of the Sigona Bowl.

Every trophy is more than a personal milestone—it’s a symbol of hope and opportunity. Through mentorship, junior clinics, and community programs, the Foundation channels Michael’s success into growth for others. Our mission is to inspire, uplift, and create lasting change through golf, proving that excellence on the course can spark positive transformation in society.
